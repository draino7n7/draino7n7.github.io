I specialize in building simulation architectures that prioritize long-term sustainment over short-term licensing. My mission is Sovereign Computing: ensuring that the tools we use to build critical infrastructure cannot be revoked, deprecated, or locked behind a subscription.

The Open Game Stack (OGS) serves three communities with one unified philosophy:

  • Indie Developers: Your one-stop launcher for game development. Download Godot, Blender, Krita, and Audacity once—then build as many projects as you want without disk bloat or version conflicts.
  • Studios: Team consistency from day one. New hires clone the repo, launch the tools, and get the exact same environment as everyone else. No more surprises. Version control for your entire creative stack.
  • Defense & Enterprise: Sovereignty guaranteed. No licensing servers. No vendor kill-switches. Audit the source code. Run offline forever. Archive the entire stack for 20+ year sustainment.

With over 20 years of experience spanning Naval Nuclear Propulsion and commercial game development, I believe mission-critical systems—whether indie projects or defense simulations—deserve tools you own, not rent.
